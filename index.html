Because many digital tools are either too general, too invasive, or simply not adapted to the realities of complex chronic illnesses. What It Can Do

  • Record: Symptoms, functional capacity, triggers, energy levels, relapses.
  • Recognize: Patterns of Post-Exertional Malaise, energy curves, and recovery windows.
  • Visualize: Time sequences, trends, subjective perceptions vs. physiological observations.
  • Structure: For doctor visits, therapeutic reflection, applications (e.g., insurance).
  • Understand: Through GPT-based input simulation â€“ always traceable, non-therapeutic, and feedback-based.
  • Export: Data packets in PDF or JSON, adapted to individual needs.

How It Works

VIRENNA is not an app in the traditional sense, but a modular, offline-ready framework that you can use, modify, and adapt. It is 100% ad-free, 100% non-tracking, 100% transparent. The system works in Markdown (readable like text), PDF (printable for doctors), and with GPT interfaces (dialogue-based insights).

Modules

  • MiMIR-GPT: interprets symptoms and provides dialogical structure
  • VISGEN-GPT: generates graphical overviews and PDF templates
  • SyncGPT: enables multi-session reflection
  • FORGE-X: supports fictional and ethical scenario play for self-clarification
  • NEXUS Core: handles versioning, data separation, and security protocols

Ethical Foundation

VIRENNA is based on the principles of dignity, autonomy, transparency, and accessibility. It does not give advice. It does not collect data. It does not profile. It does not lie.

Use Cases

  • Patients: VIRENNA supports when cognition, memory, or strength falters.
  • Caregivers: Get a clear overview instead of chaotic logs.
  • Doctors: Receive structured PDF reports with medical terminology.
  • System Stakeholders: Use ethical-by-design tech to support high-burden care scenarios.
